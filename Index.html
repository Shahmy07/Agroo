About us

We are
Agro One

A global supplier of plant-based processed tropical products, based in Sri Lanka – formerly known as Ceylon.

AGROONE supplies finest quality plant-based processed food, ethically sourced from Sri Lanka – formerly known as Ceylon, the home for world-renowned Ceylon Tea. Accredited by renowned global authorities, the entire process of AGROONE complies with profound international food safety standards.

Why us

Agro
Advantage

AGROONE, operates on its corporate ambition of making healthy, nutritious and sustainable food with wholesome goodness accessible for everyone.

Wholesome goodness. From Sri Lanka.
Committed to

Ethical Sourcing

Supporting the smallholder farmers, AGROONE sources the highest-grade nutrient-dense products made from grown by known and trusted cultivators and producers who are encouraged to practice sustainable methods of farming that help.

Profound

Food Safety & Quality Standards

AGROONE is committed to adhere to the international food safety and quality standards throughout from sourcing to processing and transportation. State-of-the-art technology is in place at the processing plant of AGROONE, where rigorous quality tests are being carried out.

Passion for

Social Responsibility

AGROONE believes that everyone and everything - from its farmers to the environment is impacted by our decisions. Envisioning to lessen the footprint on the environment is one of the prime reasons that has made AGROONE an organic and plant-based food company.

Accrediations

Sustainability

Empowering
local farmers

An empowered smallholder farmer helps make the world a better place.

AGROONE works with smallholder farmers, who are environmentally conscious and rich in knowledge handed down from generation to generation. They share a common goal with AGROONE in safeguarding the environment while facing formidable challenges and unforeseen climate changes that impact their cultivations.

AGROONE, with the aid of Government agencies conducts regular knowledge sharing sessions as a part of continuous development programmes, imparting latest technology-based know-how and scientific findings.
